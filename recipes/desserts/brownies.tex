\recipe{Brownies}

\recipesection{Recipe}

Melt butter over stove in pan.

\begin{ingredients}
\ingredientAlt{}{}{1/2}{cup}{butter}
\end{ingredients}

Remove heat then mix in cocoa and sugar.

\begin{ingredients}
\ingredientAlt{}{}{6}{tbsp}{cocoa powder}
\ingredientAlt{}{}{1}{cup}{sugar}
\end{ingredients}

Beat in eggs one at a time.

\begin{ingredients}
\ingredient{2}{}{eggs}
\end{ingredients}

Mix in remaining ingredients:

\begin{ingredients}
\ingredientAlt{}{}{1}{tsp}{vanilla extract}
\ingredientAlt{}{}{3/4}{cup}{flour}
\end{ingredients}

Optionally add a couple handfuls of chocolate chips or chopped nuts.

\begin{ingredients}
\ingredient{}{}{chocolate chips or chopped nuts}
\end{ingredients}

Pour into 8x8 glass dish.

Cook for 30 minutes at \SI{350}{Â°F}.

Let cool for 10 minutes before serving.
