\recipe{Cornbread}

Skillet cornbread based on a recipe by Kenji López-Alt \footnote{https://www.seriouseats.com/moist-and-tender-brown-butter-cornbread-recipe}.

\recipesection{Recipe}

Add butter to a 10-inch skillet and place in \SI{425}{°F} oven until melted and slightly browned.

\begin{ingredients}
\ingredient{7}{tbsp}{unsalted butter}
\end{ingredients}

In a mixing bowl, add yellow cornmeal, flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt, and sugar.

\begin{ingredients}
\ingredientAlt{130}{g}{1}{cup}{fine yellow cornmeal}
\ingredientAlt{130}{g}{1}{cup}{all-purpose flour}
\ingredientAlt{50}{g}{4}{tbsp}{sugar}
\ingredient{2}{tsp}{baking powder}
\ingredient{1}{tsp}{kosher salt}
\ingredient{1/4}{tsp}{baking soda}
\end{ingredients}

Transfer the browned butter back to the mixing bowl and add eggs, buttermilk, and sour cream.

\begin{ingredients}
\ingredient{2}{}{eggs}
\ingredientAlt{170}{g}{3/4}{cup}{sour cream}
\ingredientAlt{113}{g}{1/2}{cup}{cultured buttermilk}
\end{ingredients}

Whisk together and pour into the hot cast iron skillet coated with the remnants of the browned butter. Bake until cooked through and browned, about 20 to 25 minutes.
