
\recipe{Pumpkin Bread}

This Pumpkin Bread always turns out rich and moist. Based on a recipe by Laura Jacobson, Catherine Votaw, and Kate Klein.

\recipesection{Recipe}

Sift dry ingredients together. Mix thoroughly.

\begin{ingredients}
\ingredientAlt{476}{g}{3.5}{cup}{flour}
\ingredientAlt{600}{g}{3}{cup}{sugar}
\ingredient{2}{tsp}{baking soda}
\ingredient{1.5}{tsp}{salt}
\ingredient{3}{tsp}{cinnamon}
\ingredient{2}{tsp}{nutmeg}
\end{ingredients}

Separately, combine pumpkin, water, oil, and egg whites. Mix thoroughly.

\begin{ingredients}
\ingredient{1.5}{tsp}{vanilla}
\ingredientAlt{425}{g}{2}{cup}{pumpkin}
\ingredientAlt{158}{g}{2/3}{cup}{water}
\ingredientAlt{218}{g}{1}{cup}{vegetable oil}
\ingredient{8}{}{egg whites}
\end{ingredients}

Combine wet and dry ingredients and mix until well smooth.

Pour into 2 greased 9x5x3 inch loaf pans.

Bake in a \SI{350}{Â°F} oven for 75 minutes.

Let cool before removing from pans.
