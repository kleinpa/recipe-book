\recipe{Brownies}

\recipesection{Recipe}

Melt butter over stove in pan.

\begin{ingredients}
\ingredientAlt{113}{g}{1}{stick}{unsalted butter}
\end{ingredients}

Remove heat then mix in cocoa and sugar.

\begin{ingredients}
\ingredientAlt{32}{g}{6}{tbsp}{cocoa powder}
\ingredientAlt{198}{g}{1}{cup}{sugar}
\end{ingredients}

Beat in eggs one at a time.

\begin{ingredients}
\ingredient{2}{}{eggs}
\end{ingredients}

Mix in remaining ingredients:

\begin{ingredients}
\ingredientAlt{5}{g}{1}{tsp}{vanilla extract}
\ingredientAlt{90}{g}{3/4}{cup}{flour}
\ingredientAlt{2}{g}{1/4}{tsp}{salt}
\end{ingredients}

Optionally add chocolate chips or chopped nuts.

\begin{ingredients}
\ingredient{1}{handfull}{chocolate chips or chopped nuts}
\end{ingredients}

Pour batter into a \SI[product-units = single]{8 x 8}{} glass baking dish.

Bake for 30 minutes at \SI{350}{Â°F}.

Let cool for 10 minutes before serving.
